"Sanctify yourselves, for tomorrow the Lord will perform wonders among you." (Joshua 3:5)

"Dear Children! Also today I call you to prayer. Renew your personal prayer, and in a special way pray to the Holy Spirit to help you pray with the heart." (Medjugorje, May 25th, 2006)

Personal Prayer for Spiritual Power

1. Praise be to the Triune God, who fills heaven and earth

"Our Father in heaven, hallowed be your name" (Matthew 6:9)

Oh, God, the Creator, I praise and glorify you.
Oh, Holy Trinity, the abode of love, I praise you.
Hallelujah, Hallelujah... (Praise spontaneously)

2. Praise the indwelling Holy Trinity

(Place both hands on your chest, close your eyes, and pray fervently)

I praise you Loving Father, dwelling in me
I praise you Lord Jesus, living in me
I praise you Holy Spirit, my helper, who lives in me.
Hallelujah, Hallelujah... (Praise spontaneously)

3. Worship the Holy Trinity dwelling in our hearts

(Place both hands on your chest, close your eyes, and pray fervently)

I worship you, Father, dwelling in me,
I worship you, Jesus, dwelling in me,
I worship you, Holy Spirit, my helper, dwelling in me.
Hallelujah, Hallelujah... (Praise spontaneously)

4. Total surrender to God

"Your Kingdom come..." (Matthew 6:10)

Surrender your spirit, soul, and body to the Holy Trinity dwelling in you.

Spirit: I surrender my spirit. I submit all the wounds and impurities that are barriers to the intervention of the Holy Spirit into my spirit.

Conscience: Oh, indwelling Holy Spirit, I surrender my conscience with all its wrong convictions.

Mind: To the Holy Trinity, I surrender all the inner areas of my mind. Holy Spirit, living in me, I surrender my unconscious mind, subconscious mind, and conscious mind. I submit all the wounds imbibed when I was in my mother's womb, wounds due to the lack of parental love. I surrender all other wounds caused by dejection, grief, anxiety, worry, inferiority complex, hatred, contempt, pride, anger, jealousy, lust, greed, weakness, and other bad habits.

Senses: Surrender your sense organs to the indwelling Holy Trinity.

  • Eyes: I submit all the impurities that I have seen through my eyes. (The films, frightening scenes, pornographic films, and impure literature I have read, etc.)
  • Ears: I submit all the impure conversations, songs, stories, careless talks, and hurting words I have heard.
  • Tongue: I submit all the hurting words which have come from my mouth and all impure conversations I have made, etc.
  • Nose: I surrender all the impure senses of smell that got into me. The smell of cigarettes, alcohol, and perfumes which I crave or use to intoxicate me and the moments when I have enjoyed them.
  • Skin: I submit all the blemishes that have come through my senses of touch. I submit the moments when I have touched others or others have touched me with lust or impure thoughts. Also, the moments if I have made my own body impure by impure deeds.

Thoughts and imaginations: To the Holy Trinity dwelling in me, I surrender all the zones of impure thoughts and imaginations.

Body: Holy Spirit abiding in me, I surrender my physical body, each of my organs, muscles, blood vessels, germs, blood, each and every cell of my body, the sinful desires of the flesh, and its weaknesses.

Sexuality: Oh, Holy Spirit in me, I submit my sexuality, with all its impurities and weaknesses.

Sickness: Oh, God, I surrender all my sickness of body and mind.

Sufferings: Oh, Holy Trinity dwelling in me, I surrender all my just and unjust sufferings.

"Yet it was our infirmities that he bore, our sufferings that he endured, while we thought of him as stricken, as one smitten by God and afflicted" (Isaiah 53:4)

Other areas: Oh, God, I submit all other areas concerning me. Financial problems, difficulties in my career, anxieties of the future, all my personal relationships, parents, brothers and sisters, life partner, married life and its associated problems, children and worries about them, co-workers, etc.

5. Sanctification

"Your will be done, on earth as in heaven." (Matthew 6:10)
"This is the will of God, your holiness: that you refrain from immorality." (1 Thessalonians 4:3)

Pray to the Holy Spirit, dwelling in you, to reveal all the wounds and impurities in the submitted zones.

Oh, Holy Spirit abiding in me, reveal all the blemishes of my inner areas, i.e., spirit, soul, conscience, unconscious mind, subconscious mind, conscious mind, and inner senses (Pray in your heart). According to the revelation of incidents (impurities, hurts, and wounds), pray to Jesus to cleanse you with His Precious Blood.

Oh, Jesus! dwelling in my heart, cleanse me by your holy blood... cleanse this particular sin... and purify me. Kindly wash the stains of my sins and debts in the ocean of thy grace and purify me. Sanctify me. (Pray in this manner for some time and you will experience the cleansing process)

6. Empowerment/Grace

"Give us today our daily bread" (Matthew 6:11)

I pray to you, God, to fill me with the fruit, gifts, and charisms of the Spirit.

Fruit of the Spirit (Galatians 5:22): Fill me with the fruit of the Holy Spirit: love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, humility, and self-control.

The gifts of the Holy Spirit: (Isaiah 11:2-3) Wisdom, intelligence, knowledge, understanding, counsel, fortitude, godliness, fear of the Lord, devotion, and reverence to God.

The charisms of the Holy Spirit: (1 Corinthians 12:7-10) Fill me with your charisms. Word of wisdom, word of knowledge, gift of discerning spirits, gift of tongues, gift of interpreting tongues, gift of prophecy, gift of faith, gift of healing, gift of performing miracles, gift of ministry, gift of preaching the Gospel, and gift of intercession.

Your grace is sufficient for me. Fill me with your grace. (Pray repeatedly)

7. Forgive and Love (John 13:34)

"...And forgive us our debts, as we forgive our debtors." (Matthew 6:12)

Submit your unconscious mind and subconscious mind to the Holy Spirit dwelling in you and pray to reveal the persons whom you hate and those who hate you.

Prayer: Indwelling Holy Spirit, I humbly beg you to reveal all the persons who hate me and others whom I hate, and all the incidents which led to it. According to the revelation of the incidents and persons, visualize them in your mind and forgive them in the name of Jesus Christ. Free them from the bondage of hatred. Accept forgiveness from them and love them.

Brother/Sister, in the name of Jesus, forgive me. Free me of all bondages, in the name of the Savior, I forgive, I free you from all bondages. In the name of Jesus, I love you. Holy Spirit abiding in my heart, Spirit of unity, unite us.

8. Repentance of sin

Oh, Holy Trinity living in me, kindly reveal all my sins. Give me the realization and repentance of sin.

Have mercy on me, a sinner. Kindly give me complete remission of sins.

9. Listen to the Word of God

Loving Father, speak to me through the Word of God. (John 8:31; Matthew 12:24) (Prayerfully open the Bible and read a part from the New Testament).

10. Communicate to the Holy Trinity living in you

"But when he comes, the Spirit of Truth, he will guide you to all truth. He will not speak on his own, but he will speak what he hears, and will declare to you the things that are coming" (John 16:13)

(Place both hands on your chest, close your eyes, with full concentration, speak to the God dwelling in you)

Prayer: Oh, God abiding in me, speak to me. Thy humble servant listens.

11. Intercessory Prayer

"And do not subject us to the final test, but deliver us from the evil one." (Matthew 6:13)

Pray for others according to the inspirations of the Spirit, for the Church, for priests, parish, Gospel preachers, workers of God's ministry, for the sick, for relatives, friends, and those who have requested your prayers.

12. Thanksgiving

"In all circumstances give thanks for this is the will of God for you in Christ Jesus." (1 Thessalonians 5:17)

Give thanks to the Lord for all the blessings you have received. Accept our daily cross from the hands of the Lord and give thanks for it.

"Without Good Friday there is no Easter."

"Personal Prayer, which is as it were the very breath of the Christian, should never be neglected. There is also a need to help the faithful to rediscover the link between this personal prayer and liturgical prayer." (Ecclesia in Europa 78)
